Problem Statement

Our startup company wants to tackle the rising problem of food waste across the country and proposes a Food Waste Tracker.

Currently, restaurants are main contributors to food waste generated 22-33 billion pounds annually.

Food waste leads to landfill overflow, greenhouse emissions, and create issues globally.

Problem Solution

Our startup offers an AI-powered food waste tracking system designed to help restaurants minimize waste, cut costs, and operate more sustainably.

By leveraging smart sensors and POS systems, our solution identifies inefficiencies in food usage and provides actionable insights to optimize inventory and purchasing decisions.
